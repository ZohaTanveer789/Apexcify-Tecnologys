NexusStream | Cloud Infrastructure & Scalability

The Challenge: A global streaming platform was experiencing 40% latency spikes during peak user traffic, leading to significant churn.
Our Solution: We architected a multi-region serverless infrastructure using AWS Lambda and Terraform. By implementing an automated load-balancing protocol, we ensured the system could handle up to 1 million concurrent users without a hitch. Tech Stack: AWS, Terraform, Kubernetes, Go. The Result: * 99.99% Uptime achieved.60% Reduction in infrastructure overhead costs.

FinGuard AI | Fintech Security & ML

The Challenge: A leading fintech company needed to enhance its fraud detection capabilities while ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements.
Our Solution: We developed a machine learning model integrated with real-time data processing to identify suspicious transactions. The solution was built on a secure, scalable architecture to handle high volumes of data. Tech Stack: Python, TensorFlow, Apache Kafka, Docker. The Result: * 95% Accuracy in fraud detection.30% Reduction in false positives.

HealthSync | Interoperable Healthcare SaaS

The Challenge: A healthcare provider struggled with siloed patient data across multiple clinics, making holistic care impossible. Our Solution: We built a centralized, HIPAA-compliant SaaS dashboard that aggregates patient records via a secure API layer. We focused on a mobile-first UI to ensure doctors could access vitals on the go. Tech Stack: React, Node.js, Docker, MongoDB. The Result: * 30% Faster patient processing time.Seamless integration across 15 regional clinics.

EcoTrade | Blockchain Supply Chain

The Challenge: An ethical retail brand needed to prove the transparency of its supply chain to conscious consumers. Our Solution: We implemented a private blockchain ledger that tracks products from the raw material stage to the final storefront, providing a verifiable "digital passport" for every item. Tech Stack: Solidity, Ethereum, React Native. The Result: * Established 100% supply chain transparency. Boosted brand loyalty and consumer trust by 45%.
